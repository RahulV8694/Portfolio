Extended-Length Audio Dataset for Synthetic Voice Detection and Speaker Recognition (ELAD-SVDSR)

To be published · Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Clarkson University

This paper introduces ELAD-SVDSR, a robust dataset aimed at advancing synthetic voice (DeepFake) detection and automatic speaker recognition. It includes ~45-minute recordings from 36 participants using five different microphones, along with synthetic voice samples generated from 20 subjects using both open-source and commercial tools. The dataset supports text-dependent modeling and contains detailed metadata on speaker demographics and recording conditions. ELAD-SVDSR is poised to enable cutting-edge research in biometric security, voice authentication, and audio forensics.
